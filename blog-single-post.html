KAF Presents Child Artist of the Year 2022
Last Date 22 June 2022
Keshav Art Foundation invites all little creative minds to participate in Child Artist of the Year 2022 Online Art Competition and Exhibition. It is an International level competition and children from any country can send their entry for participation.

Participation Fee: Rs200 (nonrefundable) each artwork

Topic: WATER - Water can be shown in any form, it totally depends upon the child's creative imagination.

Medium: All mediums are acceptable for this competition. Anything and everything starting from watercolor, pencil colors, crayons, paints to waste material, any medium is acceptable.

Eligibility: This Online competition is open for the children of age 3-16yrs.

Prices and Medals (prizes mentioned below will be divided among two categories of age groups)
AgeGroup 1: 3-8yrs
AgeGroup 2: 9-16yrs

1 Grand Prize (For Best Entry) - Certificate + Trophy + Art Supplies Hamper + Promotion on Website and Social Medias

More Prizes:-

1st Position: Certificate + Gold Medal + Art Supplies Hamper
2nd Position: Certificate + Silver Medal + Art Supplies Hamper
3rd Position: Certificate + Bronze Medal + Art Supplies Hamper
5 Art Supplies Hampers as Consolation Prize

List of prizes may increase or decrease depending uponthe choice of founder and no obligations should be made against the choice of awards given by foundation.

Digital Certificate of Honor to all the participants would be emailed. Art Works of all the participants will be exhibited in an online exhibition at our website

Result: 2 July 2022

*Prizes will be couriered during August and September (please have patience and give us time as this process may take some time and provide proper address with PinCode as the certificates and medals once couriered will not be sent again).
