KAF Presents धरोहर 2
Last Date 15 April 2022
धरोहर 2 is a Virtual Art Residency & Exhibition for all the artists who are not connected with any community studio. Although, everyone can apply who can manage timings for video conferences time to time. This is an open call for the artists who are looking for networking and looking forward to meet other artists at the comfort of their own studio space. More details about how this will work will be explained after selection. Open for artists aged 20 years or above. Looking forward to sone interesting applications. This will be a one month long online residency.

Why you should apply for धरोहर 2?

  • Artists will get exposure like never before.
  • Artists will be able to interact with other artists from all over the country at the comfort of their home.
  • You never know, you might find some new friends.
  • This Online Residency is designed to work from home as we all did during the pandemic but also gain loads of knowledge with artists own practice.
  • All the participants will automatically become a part of Annual Giveaway with Rs.5000 coming in May 2022. Giveaway will only be for artists who participate in our competitions and exhibitions.
  • This residency will work as a learning opportunity for all the artists.
  • All the selected artists will get featured on our social media handles and website.
  • Hard copy of Certificate of Participation will be courierred at your respective addresses.
  • Participation fee is Rs.750 only, which is not too low and not too high.
  • There is no registration fee, you only pay if you are selected for residency come exhibition.
