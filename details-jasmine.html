Statement Of Work
Pandhi and I are entwined where he is a breathing figure as well as a journey of exploring expressions and I a traveller on that journey which is Art where Art, for me is a quaint imbibed with quintessence of breathing, and I a traveller on this journey of expressions. My work reflects the memories of “Scarlet” on a journey towards a place called “Home”, where Scarlet is a language and an emotion called “Innocence”. My approach towards art is Conceptual, Minimalist and Contemporary. My work primarily originated from spirit and nature, where my existence first felt Art, as a free flow of energy hopping between living species, irrespective of their physical appearances. Me and my works are entwined as my every work expresses a strong reflection of my existence and memories which lay as an equilibrium for a new spirit that my work depicts. Lines in my artworks are connections en routing a journey from senses towards triggering emotions. Lines and textures have a strong presence in my works as lines are the textural appearance of the spirit of my work depicting a journey in search of a happy place called home, which is not bounded by the shadow boundaries of four walls rather it’s an essence of completeness and purity.

Artist Bio
I, Jasmine Kaur, a resident of New Delhi completed my educational journey from the field of English Literature towards Fine Art as an integrated experience including Drawing, Painting, Print-Making and Sculpture. My approach towards art is Conceptual, Minimalist and Contemporary. My works are a sensitive reflection of me towards the moments where i felt art as a person “scarlet” which is “innocence”. My work focuses upon my submissive attitude towards art and how patiently i am waiting for it to draw itself. Since i am comfortable in all mediums and techniques hence i wait for my art to decide its own journey of expression. Since i have been taught under professor Mr. Ananda Moy Banerji, his ideologies and sensitivity towards nature inspired me a lot and will remain inspiring me.
