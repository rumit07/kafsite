Ayumi Adachi

Ayumi’s body of works ranges is from large - scale installation to painting. Her series displayed here is the Line series. “ 線 Line” is an installation using repurposed material and experience from previous projects to transform individual drawings into a three - dimensional object. The drawings made on translucent paper show straight lines representing a time axis. Crumpled into a ball and folded open again, the paper shows a physical memory of the crumpling. Straight lines are reborn from curves. The process of crumpling and straightening the paper is repeated until the space is completely filled up, allowing for the visual interweaving of individual drawings. Whole artwork symbolises the perceptive human mind containing the accumulation of time, experience, knowledge, history and emotions too.
