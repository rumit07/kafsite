ABOUT US /
A CREATIVE
ART GALLERY

KAF is an online art platform to support visual artists. We organise various online art competitions and art exhibitions. Since year 2018, we have successfully organised more than 15 art competitions and exhibitions. It is always a pleasure to recognise new talent with each passing exhibition. We have also organised few Instagram live sessions mene Creative Saturday and Talkative Sunday. In Creative Saturday, we had demo sessions with very skilful artists whereas in Talkative Sunday, we had talk sessions with various practicing and award winning artists.

PAINTING

Painting is the practice of applying paint, pigment, color or other medium to a solid surface.

SCULPTURE

The art of making two- or three-dimensional representative or abstract forms, especially by carving stone.

PRINT MAKING

Printmaking is an artistic process based on the principle of transferring images from a matrix onto another surface.

NEW MEDIA ART

New media art refers to artworks created with new media technologies, including digital art and virtual art.

OUR FOUNDER /
WE ARE THE BEST
Miss Nidhi Garg is the founder of Keshav Art Foundation online Art Gallery and an art practitioner specialized in drawing and paintings.She has been exhibiting at various art galleries from past few time.Her idea is to provide equal opportunities to all the art lovers whether they are qualified in art or not.Because art can not me measurd through marks.It needs to be felt.
Through KAF,she works on providing platform to visual artists and photographers to showcase their artworks online and also get rewarded for them.She believes that for an artists,continuity is very important.If you have started doing art,than you must continue to explore it further.

Portraitist of the Year 2019

Portraitist of the year was the very first national level an online art competition & Exhibition took place at Keshav Art Foundation, online platform to support young artists. 38 artworks were selected for this exhibition. Artist Mohit Beniwal, Artist Amit Baidya and child artist Ashwika Kundan were the gold medalists in this competition.

Child Artist of the Year 2018

Child Artist of the year 2018 was an online art competition and exhibition organised for little child artists of India. Many child artists participated in this competition. Child Artist Payel Roy and Junior Child Artist Reyansh were the gold medalists in this online art competition.

1st Online Drawing competition 2018

1st Online Drawing Competition & Exhibition was an open call drawing competition designed specifically for drawing mediums like pen, pencil, charcoal, printmaking, etc. Artist Seema Nadar, a dry pastel drawing artist was the jury member of this competition. 37 beautiful artworks were a part of this exhibition. Artist K Vasantha Sena was the gold medalist in this contest.
