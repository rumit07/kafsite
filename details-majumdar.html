Statement Of Work
Born on 15th January, 1989, Konnagar, Hooghly, West Bengal, India. Now I am staying at Greater Noida, UP. I completed my Master’s degree in Painting (MFA) on 2021 from Noida International University, Greater Noida, UP and Bachelor’s degree from FineArts College, Vadodara, Gujarat in painting in the year 2011. My practice carries the day-today experiences that I have gone through. The majority of work brings out the daily surroundings that I have been observing through. My early paintings are related to old architecture, ancient buildings. I am very much interested in textures like bricks, wooden textures and wall textures. Portray the self experiences as being a woman. The surroundings and daily objects which affect the mental status that has been well portrayed through my work. My foremost medium being watercolor, I also enjoy the medium.
