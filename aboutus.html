about us
WHO WE ARE /
WE CREATE ART GALLERY

KAF is an online art platform to support visual artists. We organise various online art competitions and art exhibitions. Since year 2018, we have successfully organised more than 15 art competitions and exhibitions. It is always a pleasure to recognise new talent with each passing exhibition. We have also organised few Instagram live sessions mene Creative Saturday and Talkative Sunday. In Creative Saturday, we had demo sessions with very skilful artists whereas in Talkative Sunday, we had talk sessions with various practicing and award winning artists.

PAINTING

Painting is the practice of applying paint, pigment, color or other medium to a solid surface.

SCULPTURE

The art of making two- or three-dimensional representative or abstract forms, especially by carving stone.

PRINT MAKING

Printmaking is an artistic process based on the principle of transferring images from a matrix onto another surface.

NEW MEDIA ART

New media art refers to artworks created with new media technologies, including digital art and virtual art.
